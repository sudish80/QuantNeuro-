## 🔧 TROUBLESHOOTING

**No signals generated?**
→ Check market hours (9:30-16:00 ET)
→ Check data fetching works

**All losses?**
→ Reduce position size (risk 1% instead of 2%)
→ Increase confidence threshold (0.5 instead of 0.4)
→ Check market volatility (trade when VIX < 25)

**Alpaca connection error?**
→ Verify API keys in .env
→ Check internet connection
→ Enable paper trading in account settings
